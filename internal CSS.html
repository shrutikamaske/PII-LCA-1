Understanding Procrastination

Procrastination is the act of delaying tasks due to fear, distractions, or lack of motivation.

Many people struggle with time management and discipline, leading to missed deadlines.

Quick Productivity Tips

  • Use the Pomodoro Technique
  • Break tasks into small steps
  • Set a timer for focused work
  • Avoid distractions like social media
